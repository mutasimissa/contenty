# Business Model Framework

- revenue model

## Questions
- Is the site generating leads, demos, calls, purchases, applications, or inquiries?
- What is the main conversion path?
- Is the audience technical, executive, operational, or consumer?
- What information must exist on the site to move the buyer forward?
