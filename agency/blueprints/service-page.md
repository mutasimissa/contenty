# Service Page Blueprint

## Goal

Explain a specific offer and convert qualified interest.

## Recommended sections

1. Hero with offer clarity
2. Customer problem
3. Solution summary
4. Feature / capability blocks
5. Process or delivery model
6. Outcomes and proof
7. FAQ
8. CTA

## Inputs required

- business model
- page brief
- SEO brief

## Internal linking

- CTA links to contact page
- Related services link to other service pages
- Proof section links to case studies
- FAQ links to main FAQ page

## Accessibility

- H1 for service name, H2 for each section
- Feature blocks use semantic list markup
- CTA buttons have descriptive text
