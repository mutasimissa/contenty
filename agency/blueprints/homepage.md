# Homepage Blueprint

## Goal

State the business clearly, establish trust quickly, and route the visitor to
the right next step.

## Recommended sections

1. Hero
2. Problem and stakes
3. Solution overview
4. Key offerings
5. Differentiators
6. Proof / trust
7. Industries or use cases
8. FAQ or objections
9. Final CTA

## Inputs required

- brand strategy
- value proposition
- offer design
- proof assets
- CTA strategy

## Internal linking

- Hero CTA links to contact page
- Solutions overview links to solutions page
- Industries section links to industry pages
- Proof section links to case studies
- Final CTA links to contact page

## Accessibility

- H1 in hero, H2 for each major section
- Skip-to-content link as first focusable element
- All images have descriptive alt text
- CTA buttons have descriptive text (not "Click here")
