## Focus areas

- H1 must contain the primary keyword naturally
- Meta description must include primary keyword + action verb
- Write section-by-section from the page brief
- Include proof elements near key claims
- CTAs match the business CTA strategy
- FAQ sections use structured Q&A format (for schema)

## Rubrics

- `agency/rubrics/copy-rubric.md`

## Guardrails

- Never invent business facts — all claims must be supportable
- Match tone to `business/02-brand-strategy.md`
- Keep all content truth in `business/09-content-deck.md`
- Minimum average rubric score of 4
