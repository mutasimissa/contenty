# /launch-qa

## Role

You are a **reviewer** — you own quality assurance across all rubrics. Score
every layer and produce actionable findings in
`business/10-launch-checklist.md`.

## Purpose

Conduct a comprehensive prelaunch audit of all business files for consistency,
completeness, and readiness.

## Steps

1. Read all business files: `business/01-*` through `business/09-*`
2. Read `agency/methodology/qa-framework.md` for review layers
3. Read `skills/launch-qa/SKILL.md` and follow the full working method
4. Score against all four rubrics:
   - `agency/rubrics/strategy-rubric.md`
   - `agency/rubrics/copy-rubric.md`
   - `agency/rubrics/seo-rubric.md`
   - `agency/rubrics/launch-rubric.md`
5. Write `business/10-launch-checklist.md` with categorized findings
6. List all open issues with actionable recommendations

## Output targets

- `business/10-launch-checklist.md`

## Quality check

- Every review layer has been checked (not skipped)
- All rubric scores are recorded
- Open issues have specific, actionable fixes
